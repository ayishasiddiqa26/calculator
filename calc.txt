This project describes calculator operations using text files.
Addition operation is represented by the symbol '+'
example : 2+3=5
The addition of two whole numbers results in the total or sum of those values combined.
Subtraction operation is represented by the symbol '-' 
Example: 10-5=5 
The subtraction of two whole numbers results in the difference obtained by taking one value away from the other.
Multiplication operation is represented by '*' 
Example : 2*5=10 
The multiplication of two whole numbers results in the product obtained by combining those values through repeated addition.
